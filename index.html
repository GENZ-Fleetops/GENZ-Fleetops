Operations Flow

From Booking
to Profit

Every step automated, tracked and optimized. Your team focuses on service — GENZ FleetOps handles the system.

Complete Loop
01 Customer Acqusition (Ads · WhatsApp · Web)
02 Booking + Auto Pricing
03 Payment Processed
04 CRM Records + Dispatch Assigns
05 Driver Executes Trip
06 Profit Tracked ✓
01
Customer Acquisition
Instagram, Facebook Ads, website, and WhatsApp inquiries funnel leads to your booking page. Corporate clients onboarded directly.
02
Smart Booking Form
Customer enters name, mobile, pickup/drop, date & time. Selects vehicle type: Sedan, SUV, Luxury, Van or Bus.
03
Auto Price Generation
System calculates fare using route-based or distance-based pricing engine. No manual quoting. No human error.
04
Multi-Gateway Payment
Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Tabby, Tamara, and PayPal. Payment confirmed, Booking ID generated, status = Confirmed.
05
CRM Intake & Dispatch
Booking auto-flows into CRM. Operations assigns driver + vehicle. Driver receives trip via app with navigation link.
06
Trip Execution
Driver accepts, navigates via Google Maps / Waze / 2GIS, starts trip, completes journey. Customer notified at each stage.
07
Profit Calculated
Revenue minus driver cost, fuel, Salik, parking, and vendor costs = net profit per trip. Instant. Automatic. Accurate.
